Return Policy

ELIVAAS offers a simple and transparent return and cancellation approach for villa bookings.

1. Cancellation Window

Bookings can be canceled within the cancellation period stated at the time of reservation. Refund eligibility depends on the booking terms.

2. Refunds

If a booking is eligible for refund, the amount will be processed back to the original payment method within the stated timeline.

3. Non-Refundable Charges

Some bookings, promotional offers, or special packages may be non-refundable or partially refundable as per policy.

4. Changes to Booking

Guests may request date changes depending on availability and the terms of the booking.

5. Contact Support

For booking assistance, please contact our support team at hello@elivaas.com.
